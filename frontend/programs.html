Our Programs

ReadForward runs comprehensive initiatives to promote literacy and education in underserved communities. Explore our main programs designed to create lasting impact.

Library Building

Creating fully-stocked libraries in schools to provide students with access to quality reading material and learning resources.

  • Complete library setup
  • Book collections curation
  • Reading space design
  • Digital resources integration

Reading Programs

Organizing reading challenges, story sessions, and workshops to encourage literacy and develop reading habits.

  • Reading challenges
  • Author visits
  • Book clubs
  • Reading workshops

Exchange Programs

Fundraising initiatives through student exchange programs to support library building projects and cultural exchange.

  • Cultural exchange
  • Language learning
  • Global perspective
  • Community building
